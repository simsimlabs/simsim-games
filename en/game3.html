üéÆ Worm.io Strategy Guide

Worm.io is a popular snake-style IO survival game. Start as a small worm, eat scattered light dots to grow massive. Defeat other players by making them crash into your body, then eat their remains to grow even faster!

üïπÔ∏è Controls

  • PC: The worm follows your mouse cursor. Click to boost speed.
  • Mobile: Use the left joystick to move and the right lightning button to boost.
  • Rules: If your head touches another worm's body, you die. Touching your own body is safe.

üèÜ Pro Tips

1. Boost Wisely: Boosting consumes your length but allows you to cut off enemies and secure a kill.

2. Encircle: Once you are long enough, surround smaller enemies to trap them until they crash into you.

3. Scavenge: When a giant worm dies, it drops a lot of mass. Be quick to eat it, but watch out for other scavengers!
